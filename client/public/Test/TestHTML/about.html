About Us

Welcome to Look-Book, an open space where you can view art from all periods of time, from all groups of people.

Look-Book is much more than an app for swiping famous pieces of art. Instead, it is a personal journalling application where you are encouraged to reflect on a piece of art that you find appealing. Recent studies have consistently proven the benefit of viewing art and reflecting on its significance to individuals and communities. This is a key function of museums and other cultural institutions. Look-Book makes this function available to you on your mobile device. Click below to log in or sign up for the application.
